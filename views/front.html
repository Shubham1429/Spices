Our Services

~ Delicious foods ~

Punjabi Catering

Our Punjabi specialties whether non-vegetarian or vegetarian are prepared using class 1 ingredients and expert knowledge, for which we continue to receive customer recommendations today.

Vegetarian Catering

We endeavor to observe religious requirements and preferences. Our vegetarian catering is very well appreciated as are our preparation methods. We specialize in without onion-garlic food also.

Chinese Catering

Chinese food very much compliments Indian food. We have many clients who request a mixed menu so that guests have the choice to combine the two if they wish.

Corporate Catering

Finding the right caterer for your corporate catering is the deal clincher for a successful event. Food is the one feature of the event that is sure to be commented on and remembered by every guest.

Event Catering

What began as event catering for Indian weddings and other Indian family celebrations has evolved into an events catering company which can organize the most logistically challenging of events.

Party Catering

We are equally proficient in organizing events in new locations and will liaise with the venue to ensure that the venue team has every confidence in the abilities of our company.

Bar Service

Our professional, down to earth, smart bartenders serve one of a kind drink recipe with class and sophistication. Each bar catering package is personalized to suit your specific theme or event.

Budget Parties

Your banqueting menu will be tailor-made as per your specific requirements. Our banqueting selector is suitably trained to enable you to choose from many appetizing dishes that will suit your palate.

Event Services

We rise to the challenges of working in new venues, especially those where service of Indian food may be a first time experience. Whether it is a special family event or a corporate entertaining event, the Blend spice6 team has the knowledge and flair to make any event absolutely bespoke and memorable.

Wine and Cheese Party

Are you having a wine and cheese party? Choose one of our delightful and exceptionally appealing cheese platters for your upcoming occasion.

At Blend Spice6 Catering we believe that using fresh of the freshest ingredients produces the best food experience ever.
